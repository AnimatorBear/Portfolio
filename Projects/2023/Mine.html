Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a game where you press a tile and it has a number , the number is the amount of bombs touching that tile. If you touch a bomb you lose. Every tile touches 8 tiles. You can place flags on bombs to mark their location and to make sure you cant press them anymore

This version of minesweeper has a small story: The entire reason it was made was because a teacher said we (me and a classmate) werent allowed to play minesweeper during the lessons , and that it was only allowed if we were making minesweeper. So we made minesweeper. In 2 days. (See classmate's version on the itch.io page)
