Race Game

The Race Game is a singleplayer racing game against 7 AI cars. You have 2 maps to choose from and around these maps are powerups , which can stun enemies , speed you up , ect. The Race Game also has a shop with upgrade you can buy with coins you earn throughout the races. Gain achievements and skins for doing certain things.

The Race Game is the third project I made at school and it was mostly written by me , unlike the previous two projects. I personally didnt really like the racing part of the race game so I focussed on things outside of the racing. Playerprefs (and Saving stuff between scenes in general) were a main theme for myself while making this so I used them a lot (Shop , Achievements , Settings and skins)
