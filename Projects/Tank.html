Tank Game

The tank game is a 2d pvp game that you can play on 1 device. It has a turn based play style in normal mode with different types of attacks that you can choose from, ranging for a normal bullet to a firework. If you dont want to pvp you can also play Boss Mode , which you can always move in , and have to work together to defeat the boss

This was my second ever project , which was made in 6-ish weeks. I tried to focus on features a lot but it turned out with a lot of confusing things that I would have to explain for people to understand. The only "stage" of boss mode that is actually good is the last stage. There were no big difficulties in development
